# EXPANSION BATCH 001

## Purpose

This batch adds 20 terms that are both high-value and relatively safe to encode
 under the current house style.

The batch focuses on:

- wholesome and unwholesome root language
- dependent-origination compounds
- internal/external contemplative vocabulary
- noble versus ordinary person-classification language
- friendship and ethical-support compounds
- framework compounds for awakening factors and faculties/strengths

## Terms Added

Major:

- `kusala-mula`
- `akusala-mula`
- `phassa-samudaya`
- `vinnana-thiti`
- `ajjhatta`
- `bahiddha`
- `anariya`
- `ariya-puggala`

Minor:

- `asappurisa-dhamma`
- `kalyanamittata`
- `papamittata`
- `lobha-mula`
- `dosa-mula`

- `indriya-bala`

## Review Notes

The major entries are intentionally draft-status rule-bearing entries. They are
 structured enough to reduce drift immediately, but they still need normal
 editorial review before being promoted.

The minor entries are intentionally lighter and should be upgraded only if real
 translation work shows they need fuller rule-bearing treatment.
