# DN 15 Translation Notes

This document is the companion rationale for
[DN 15: Mahānidāna Sutta](dn15-mahanidana-sutta.md). The main translation is
the primary study surface; this file records debated translation choices,
source-audit calls, and edition notes that govern it.

## Source Base

- Primary source: the Pali text of DN 15 as segmented in SuttaCentral's Bilara
  data.
- Control surface: the repository's current term policy, especially
  `paṭiccasamuppāda`, `idappaccayatā`, `paccaya`, `jarāmaraṇa`, `jāti`,
  `bhava`, `upādāna`, `kāmupādāna`, `diṭṭhupādāna`, `sīlabbatupādāna`,
  `attavādupādāna`, `taṇhā`, `kāmataṇhā`, `bhavataṇhā`, `vibhavataṇhā`,
  `vedanā`, `sukhavedanā`, `dukkhavedanā`, `adukkhamasukhavedanā`, `phassa`,
  `nāmarūpa`, `viññāṇa`, `rūpa`, `saññā`, `paññatti`, `diṭṭhi`, `anusaya`,
  `sakkāya`, `atta`, `yathābhūtaṃ pajānāti`, `viññāṇaṭṭhiti`, `āyatana`,
  `ākāsānañcāyatana`, `viññāṇañcāyatana`, `ākiñcaññāyatana`,
  `nevasaññānāsaññāyatana`, `saññāvedayitanirodha`, `vimutti`, `āsava`,
  `khaya`, `cetovimutti`, `paññāvimutti`, and `ubhatobhāga-vimutti`.
- Working method: the discourse was governed as one argument about the
  conditioned boundary of this state of being, rather than as a metaphysical
  account of a knowing-substance or a loose cosmological catalog.

## Governing Decisions

- `paṭiccasamuppāda` remains `dependent arising`.
- `idappaccayā` and `paccaya` are kept in conditional language:
  `because of this conditionality` and `with X as condition`.
- `viññāṇa` remains `knowing`, including the womb-descent lines and the
  reciprocal `nāmarūpa` / `viññāṇa` hinge. The English does not shift to a
  witness-like noun.
- `nāmarūpa` remains `name-and-form`.
- The summary chain keeps the same forward-link voice as the existing SN 12.2
  surface: `with X as condition, Y`.
- `upādāna` remains `taking personally`, while the four kinds of upādāna keep
  their compound records: `sensual clinging`, `taking views personally`,
  `clinging to rules and observances`, and `taking self-doctrine personally`.
- `taṇhā` remains `ignorant wanting`, and the threefold sequence is rendered
  `ignorant wanting for sensuality`, `ignorant wanting for becoming`, and
  `ignorant wanting for non-becoming`.
- `vedanā` remains `felt experience` in the main process language. The three
  named feeling registers use the existing minor records: `pleasant feeling`,
  `painful feeling`, and `mixed feeling`.
- `dukkha` remains `dissatisfaction`, including the `whole heap of
  dissatisfaction` formula.
- `parinibbāyati` is rendered `personally reaches final nibbāna`, preserving
  the parinibbāna family rather than replacing it with generic death or
  annihilation language.
- `viññāṇaṭṭhiti` is rendered `station of knowing`, following the reviewed
  compound policy.
- `vimokkha` is rendered `release` in the eight-release section, keeping it
  within the release family rather than turning the list into mystical escape
  language.
- `ubhatobhāga-vimutti` is rendered `two-sided release`, following the reviewed
  minor record.

## Re-audit Calls

- The opening rebuke to Ānanda keeps the direct warning: dependent arising is
  deep even when it appears clear to him.
- The string-and-grass simile is kept close to the image because it explains
  practical entanglement, not intellectual complexity alone.
- The destination series uses local destination records where available:
  `woeful state`, `bad destination`, `downfall state`, and `saṃsāra`.
- The repeated proof refrain renders `hetu`, `nidāna`, `samudaya`, and
  `paccaya` as `cause`, `source`, `origin`, and `condition`. This is
  document-local but intentionally stable throughout DN 15.
- The conflict sequence after felt experience is rendered as:
  `searching`, `gain`, `evaluation`, `desire and passion`, `fixation`,
  `possession`, `stinginess`, and `guarding`. The current lexicon already
  controls `lābha`, `chandarāga`, and `macchariya`; the other English choices
  are document-local and should be promoted to supporting entries only if they
  recur as drift risks in later surfaces.
- `ime dve dhammā dvayena vedanāya ekasamosaraṇā bhavanti` is rendered
  `these two dhammas meet at the two aspects of felt experience`. The line is
  kept compact because the root itself is compact and the exact referent of
  `two dhammas` should not be over-explained in the main translation.
- `adhivacanasamphassa` and `paṭighasamphassa` are rendered
  `designation-contact` and `resistance-contact`. This keeps the line tied to
  both the designation family and the existing `paṭigha` policy.
- `adhivacanapatha`, `niruttipatha`, and `paññattipatha` are rendered
  `path of designation`, `path of expression`, and `path of concept`. The
  sequence is about the range within which this state of being can be
  designated, not about abstract philosophy of language.
- The womb-descent lines keep `knowing` as a conditioned participant in
  name-and-form. The translation does not treat knowing as a transmigrating
  self.
- The self-designation section keeps `designate` language throughout, so
  `atta` is not handled as an already-established entity.
- The formula `atathaṃ ... tathattāya upakappessāmi` is rendered `Although it
  is not like that, I will make it be like that`. The awkwardness is retained
  because the line matters as a projective act of self-designation.
- The Tathāgata-after-death alternatives follow the existing DN 2 wording:
  exists, does not exist, both exists and does not exist, neither exists nor
  does not exist.
- Obscure cosmological names such as `Abhassara` and `Subhakiṇha` are left
  close to the Pali, consistent with the MN 1 policy.
- `yathābhūta` language in the stations section is kept as `as they have come
  to be`, preserving the conditioned-process force rather than flattening it
  into generic objectivity.

## Practice Clarifications

- DN 15 does not lengthen dependent arising into metaphysics. It narrows the
  range within which birth, ageing and dying, designation, concept, and the
  round can be found.
- The reciprocal name-and-form / knowing structure is the main control point.
  Knowing conditions name-and-form, and name-and-form conditions knowing; the
  discourse refuses a one-way substance story.
- The contact analysis matters because it shows contact depending on
  designation and resistance structures within name-and-form. Contact is not
  treated as a bare collision between independent things.
- The conflict sequence shows how felt experience can branch into searching,
  gain, possession, guarding, and social harm. It is not a separate moral essay
  detached from dependent arising.
- The felt-experience self-analysis blocks three common self-views: felt
  experience as self, a non-feeling self, and a self that owns or has felt
  experience.
- The seven stations of knowing and two fields are governed as conditioned
  places where knowing stands, not as final attainments.
- The eight releases culminate in two-sided release only when the outflows have
  worn away and release of mind and release through discernment have been
  personally realized in this very life.

## Editorial Presentation

- The main translation file uses section headings so the conditional chain,
  proof refrains, self-designation analysis, stations of knowing, and releases
  can function as a study text.
- Those headings are editorial aids, not claims that such headings stand in the
  root text itself.
- The repeated proof refrain is translated in full where it carries doctrinal
  pressure and compressed only where the Pali itself abbreviates the repetition.
- The stations-of-knowing delight test is summarized for the middle members
  because the root uses ellipsis there.

## Edition Status
