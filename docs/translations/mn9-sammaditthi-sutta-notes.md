# MN 9 Translation Notes

This document is the companion rationale for
[MN 9: Sammādiṭṭhi Sutta](mn9-sammaditthi-sutta.md). The main translation is
the primary study surface; this file records debated translation choices,
source-audit calls, and edition notes that govern it.

## Source Base

- Primary source: the Pali text of MN 9 as segmented in SuttaCentral's Bilara
  data.
- Control surface: the repository's current term policy, especially
  `samma-ditthi`, `ditthi`, `ariyasavaka`, `aveccappasada`, `pajanati`,
  `akusala`, `akusala-mula`, `kusala`, `kusala-mula`, `lobha`, `dosa`,
  `moha`, `alobha`, `adosa`, `amoha`, `ahara`, `kabalinkara-ahara`,
  `phassa`, `manosancetana-ahara`, `vinnana`, `dukkha`, `tanha`, `upadana`,
  `bhava`, `jati`, `jaramarana`, `salayatana`, `namarupa`, `sankhara`,
  `avijja`, `asava`, and the generated dependent-arising and path-factor
  control sheets.
- Working method: the dialogue frame, the repeated right-view refrain, and the
  analytical sequence from roots through outflows are governed together so MN 9
  reads as one extended analysis rather than as a collection of detached lists.

## Governing Decisions

- `Sammādiṭṭhi` is rendered `Right View`.
- `ariyasāvaka` remains `noble disciple`.
- `ujugatāssa diṭṭhi` is rendered `whose view has become direct`, keeping the
  straightening force without replacing the live `diṭṭhi` default.
- `aveccappasāda` is rendered `verified confidence`.
- `pajānāti` is rendered `discerns` throughout the analytical frame.
- `akusala` and `kusala` remain `unwholesome` and `wholesome`, with
  `akusalamūla` and `kusalamūla` rendered as `root of the unwholesome` and
  `root of the wholesome`.
- The root triads keep the live defaults: `greed`, `aversion`, `delusion`,
  `non-greed`, `non-aversion`, and `clarity`.
- The four-truth section stays aligned with the SN 56.11 and MN 141 surfaces:
  `dissatisfaction`, `origin`, `quenching`, and `path of practice`.
- The dependent-arising links stay aligned with SN 12.2: `name-and-form`,
  `six fields of experience`, `felt experience`, `ignorant wanting`, `taking
  personally`, `becoming`, `birth`, and `ageing and dying`.
- The outflow close stays aligned with MN 2: `sensual outflow`, `outflow of
  becoming`, `outflow of ignorance`, and `quenching of outflows`.

## Re-audit Calls

- The translation keeps `friends` for `āvuso` because the speaker is Sāriputta
  addressing fellow bhikkhus, not the Buddha addressing disciples as `bhante`.
- The opening right-view formula is repeated in full where it establishes a
  new major section. Later repetitive material is grouped under a stated
  refrain because the root itself abbreviates the recurring question-and-answer
  frame with ellipsis.
- `saddhamma` is rendered `true Dhamma` in the opening formula so the line does
  not become abstract `doctrine` language.
- The repeated liberation refrain keeps the sequence readable while preserving
  the live policies for underlying tendency, resistance, view-and-conceit,
  ignorance, clear knowledge, and the end of dissatisfaction.
- The ten unwholesome and wholesome courses are rendered as practical conduct,
  speech, and mind items rather than as legalistic categories.
- The nutriment section keeps `knowing` as the fourth nutriment, preserving the
  repository's `viññāṇa` policy while still making the list readable.
- The four-truth section is deliberately close to SN 56.11 and MN 141 because
  MN 9 should stabilize those formulas in analytical dialogue prose, not reopen
  them.
- The dependent-arising analyses use `origin` and `quenching` because MN 9 is
  asking for each item as a fourfold analysis, not reciting the forward chain
  as a `with X as condition, Y` formula.
- The final two analyses make the reciprocal pressure between ignorance and
  outflows explicit without collapsing either term into generic defilement.

## Practice Clarifications

- MN 9 defines right view not as a single opinion but as repeatedly discerning
  a domain, its origin, its quenching, and the path of practice leading to that
  quenching.
- The root analysis matters because right view first appears in ethical and
  practical discrimination, not as detached metaphysical correctness.
- The nutriment analysis matters because it connects dependent arising to the
  ways experience is sustained.
- The four-truth analysis matters because MN 9 uses the same truth-family
  surface inside a longer Sariputta exposition.
- The dependent-arising analyses matter because the discourse works backward
  through the chain, forcing each link to be read as something discerned with
  its origin and quenching.
- The outflow close matters because ignorance and outflows condition each other
  in this analytical surface, while MN 2 controls the practical methods for
  restraining and abandoning outflows.

## Editorial Presentation

- The main translation file uses section headings so the long sequence can
  function as a study text.
- Those headings are editorial aids, not claims that such headings stand in the
  root text itself.
- The repeated question "might there be another way" is translated fully at
  the first recurrence and then summarized where the root abbreviates.
- The path list is written out where it anchors a major section and kept
  compact where repeating it would obscure the analytical movement.
