## Translation

### The Setting

At Savatthi.

"Bhikkhus, I will explain what feeds and what starves the five distractions and
the seven awakening factors. Listen and attend well. I will speak."

"Yes, Bhante," the bhikkhus replied.

The Blessed One said this:

### What Feeds The Distractions

"And what feeds the arising of sensual distraction when it has not yet arisen,
or, if it has arisen, its increase and expansion?

There is the attractive sign. Repeated unwise attention there feeds the arising
of sensual distraction when it has not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its
increase and expansion.

"And what feeds the arising of aversive distraction when it has not yet arisen,
or, if it has arisen, its increase and expansion?

There is the sign of resistance. Repeated unwise attention there feeds the
arising of aversive distraction when it has not yet arisen, or, if it has
arisen, its increase and expansion.

"And what feeds the arising of dull distraction when it has not yet arisen, or,
if it has arisen, its increase and expansion?

There are discontent, sluggishness, yawning, after-meal drowsiness, and the
sinking of the feeling mind. Repeated unwise attention there feeds the arising
of dull distraction when it has not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its
increase and expansion.

"And what feeds the arising of agitated distraction when it has not yet arisen,
or, if it has arisen, its increase and expansion?

There is the feeling mind's unrest. Repeated unwise attention there feeds the
arising of agitated distraction when it has not yet arisen, or, if it has
arisen, its increase and expansion.

"And what feeds the arising of uncertain distraction when it has not yet
arisen, or, if it has arisen, its increase and expansion?

There are qualities that support uncertainty. Repeated unwise attention there
feeds the arising of uncertain distraction when it has not yet arisen, or, if
it has arisen, its increase and expansion.

### What Feeds The Awakening Factors

"And what feeds the arising of the remembering awakening factor when it has not
yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and fulfillment?

There are qualities that support the remembering awakening factor. Repeated
wise attention there feeds the arising of the remembering awakening factor when
it has not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and fulfillment.

"And what feeds the arising of the discernment-of-qualities awakening factor
when it has not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and
fulfillment?

There are qualities that are skillful and unskillful, blameworthy and
blameless, low and refined, dark and bright counterparts. Repeated wise
attention there feeds the arising of the discernment-of-qualities awakening
factor when it has not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and
fulfillment.

"And what feeds the arising of the energy awakening factor when it has not yet
arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and fulfillment?

There are the energy of getting started, the energy of stepping forward, and
the energy of pressing on. Repeated wise attention there feeds the arising of
the energy awakening factor when it has not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen,
its development and fulfillment.

"And what feeds the arising of the delight awakening factor when it has not yet
arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and fulfillment?

There are qualities that support the delight awakening factor. Repeated wise
attention there feeds the arising of the delight awakening factor when it has
not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and fulfillment.

"And what feeds the arising of the relaxation awakening factor when it has not
yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and fulfillment?

There are bodily relaxation and relaxation of the feeling mind. Repeated wise
attention there feeds the arising of the relaxation awakening factor when it
has not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and fulfillment.

"And what feeds the arising of the mental composure awakening factor when it
has not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and fulfillment?

There are the calm sign and the sign of non-scatter. Repeated wise attention
there feeds the arising of the mental composure awakening factor when it has
not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and fulfillment.

"And what feeds the arising of the dynamic-balance awakening factor when it has
not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and fulfillment?

There are qualities that support the dynamic-balance awakening factor. Repeated
wise attention there feeds the arising of the dynamic-balance awakening factor
when it has not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and
fulfillment.

### What Starves The Distractions

"And what starves the arising of sensual distraction when it has not yet
arisen, or, if it has arisen, its increase and expansion?

There is the unattractive sign. Repeated wise attention there starves the
arising of sensual distraction when it has not yet arisen, or, if it has
arisen, its increase and expansion.

"And what starves the arising of aversive distraction when it has not yet
arisen, or, if it has arisen, its increase and expansion?

There is the liberation of mind through friendliness. Repeated wise attention
there starves the arising of aversive distraction when it has not yet arisen,
or, if it has arisen, its increase and expansion.

"And what starves the arising of dull distraction when it has not yet arisen,
or, if it has arisen, its increase and expansion?

There are the energy of getting started, the energy of stepping forward, and
the energy of pressing on. Repeated wise attention there starves the arising of
dull distraction when it has not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its
increase and expansion.

"And what starves the arising of agitated distraction when it has not yet
arisen, or, if it has arisen, its increase and expansion?

There is the settling of the feeling mind. Repeated wise attention there
starves the arising of agitated distraction when it has not yet arisen, or, if
it has arisen, its increase and expansion.

"And what starves the arising of uncertain distraction when it has not yet
arisen, or, if it has arisen, its increase and expansion?

There are qualities that are skillful and unskillful, blameworthy and
blameless, low and refined, dark and bright counterparts. Repeated wise
attention there starves the arising of uncertain distraction when it has not
yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its increase and expansion.

### What Starves The Awakening Factors

"And what starves the arising of the remembering awakening factor when it has
not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and fulfillment?

There are qualities that support the remembering awakening factor. Repeated
non-attention there starves the arising of the remembering awakening factor
when it has not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and
fulfillment.

"And what starves the arising of the discernment-of-qualities awakening factor
when it has not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and
fulfillment?

There are qualities that are skillful and unskillful, blameworthy and
blameless, low and refined, dark and bright counterparts. Repeated
non-attention there starves the arising of the discernment-of-qualities
awakening factor when it has not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its
development and fulfillment.

"And what starves the arising of the energy awakening factor when it has not
yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and fulfillment?

There are the energy of getting started, the energy of stepping forward, and
the energy of pressing on. Repeated non-attention there starves the arising of
the energy awakening factor when it has not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen,
its development and fulfillment.

"And what starves the arising of the delight awakening factor when it has not
yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and fulfillment?

There are qualities that support the delight awakening factor. Repeated
non-attention there starves the arising of the delight awakening factor when it
has not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and fulfillment.

"And what starves the arising of the relaxation awakening factor when it has
not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and fulfillment?

There are bodily relaxation and relaxation of the feeling mind. Repeated
non-attention there starves the arising of the relaxation awakening factor when
it has not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and fulfillment.

"And what starves the arising of the mental composure awakening factor when it
has not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and fulfillment?

There are the calm sign and the sign of non-scatter. Repeated non-attention
there starves the arising of the mental composure awakening factor when it has
not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and fulfillment.

"And what starves the arising of the dynamic-balance awakening factor when it
has not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and fulfillment?

There are qualities that support the dynamic-balance awakening factor. Repeated
non-attention there starves the arising of the dynamic-balance awakening factor
when it has not yet arisen, or, if it has arisen, its development and
fulfillment."

The first discourse is finished.
