# Decision Record Template

Use this template when a term-family decision is too important to leave only in
commit history or scattered notes.

## Title

Short decision name, for example: `Keep nirodha -> quenching as the house
default`.

## Date

Use ISO format: `YYYY-MM-DD`.

## Scope

List the affected headwords, compounds, formulas, or corpus slices.

## Decision

State the policy clearly in one or two paragraphs.

## Why

- What drift or doctrinal confusion is being prevented?
- Why is this better than the main alternatives?
- What authority layer governs the decision?

## Evidence

- Relevant OSF source material
- Dhammarato or Buddhadasa support
- Canonical examples or formula lines
- Existing term entries that must stay aligned

## Consequences

- Which term files need revision now?
- Which downstream tooling or translation memory behavior changes?
- What review checkpoints should future editors apply?

## Follow-Up

- Documentation to update
- Terms or compounds still needing alignment
- Open questions that remain intentionally unresolved
