# Sense-Fields Contact Interface Sheet

- `āyatana` stays `field` across the family and blocks silent drift back to `base` or `organ` language.
- `saḷāyatana` stays `six fields of experience` and should remain readable as the experiential field structure from which contact can arise.
- `phassa` stays `contact` as the event-term, not the feeling that follows and not mere physical touch.
- `saḷāyatanapaccayā phasso` -> `with the six fields of experience as condition, contact` keeps the family linked at the chain level.
- `phassapaccayā vedanā` -> `with contact as condition, felt experience` keeps contact distinct from felt experience.
