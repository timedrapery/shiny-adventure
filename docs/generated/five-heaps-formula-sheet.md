# Five Heaps Formula Sheet

- `pañcime, bhikkhave, khandhā` -> these, bhikkhus, are the five heaps
- `katame ca, bhikkhave, pañcupādānakkhandhā` -> and what, bhikkhus, are the five clung-to heaps
- `yaṃ kiñci rūpaṃ ... vedanā ... saññā ... saṅkhārā ... viññāṇaṃ` -> whatever form ... felt experience ... recognition ... putting-together activities ... knowing
- `katamo ca, bhikkhave, sakkāyo, pañcupādānakkhandhā` -> and what, bhikkhus, is identity, the five clung-to heaps
- `bhāro have pañcakkhandhā` -> the five heaps are indeed a burden
- `pañcupādānakkhandhesu assādo ca ādīnavo ca nissaraṇaṃ ca` -> the gratification, danger, and escape in the five clung-to heaps
