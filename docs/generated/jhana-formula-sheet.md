# Jhana Formula Sheet

## First mental theme

- `vivekaja-piti-sukha` -> delight and ease born of seclusion

## Second mental theme

- `avitakka-avicara` -> without thinking and pondering
- `ajjhatta-sampasadana` -> inner clarity
- `cetaso-ekodibhava` -> unification of mind
- `samadhija-piti-sukha` -> delight and ease born of samadhi

## Third mental theme

- `upekkha-sukha` -> dynamic balance and ease

## Fourth mental theme

- `upekkha-satiparisuddha` -> purified by dynamic balance and remembering
- `upekkha-satiparisuddhi` -> purity of remembering through dynamic balance
