# Jhana Sequence Sheet

- `sammā-samādhi` is governed in this repository as the first, second, third, and fourth `jhāna`.
- Keep `jhāna` as `mental theme` across the fourfold sequence unless a controlled technical reason requires the Pali.

## paṭhama-jhāna

- Default: `first mental theme`
- Notes: The first jhāna in the standard fourfold sequence of ariya sammā samādhi.
- Linked formulas: `vivekaja-piti-sukha` -> delight and ease born of seclusion

## dutiya-jhāna

- Default: `second mental theme`
- Notes: The second jhāna in the standard fourfold sequence of ariya sammā samādhi.
- Linked formulas: `avitakka-avicara` -> without thinking and pondering, `ajjhatta-sampasadana` -> inner clarity, `cetaso-ekodibhava` -> unification of mind, `samadhija-piti-sukha` -> delight and ease born of mental composure

## tatiya-jhāna

- Default: `third mental theme`
- Notes: The third jhāna in the standard fourfold sequence of ariya sammā samādhi.
- Linked formulas: `upekkha-sukha` -> dynamic balance and ease

## catuttha-jhāna

- Default: `fourth mental theme`
- Notes: The fourth jhāna in the standard fourfold sequence of ariya sammā samādhi.
- Linked formulas: `upekkha-satiparisuddha` -> purified by dynamic balance and remembering, `upekkha-satiparisuddhi` -> purity of remembering through dynamic balance
