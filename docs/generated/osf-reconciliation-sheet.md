# OSF Reconciliation Sheet

| Term | Repo default | OSF-facing usage | Classification | Governance decision |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| suññatā | emptiness | OSF-facing materials support emptiness, while Buddhadasa-line prose also uses voidness and void-mind language. | ALIGN | Keep emptiness as the default, tolerate voidness as a controlled alternate, refuse nihilist and mystical drift. |
| nibbāna | nibbāna | OSF and Buddhadasa-facing materials strongly use cooling, coolness, and cooling down in explanation. | TOLERATE ALTERNATE | Keep nibbāna untranslated by default, tolerate cooling-language as explanatory OSF-facing gloss, refuse mystical and absolutized drift. |
| nirodha | quenching | OSF and Dhammarato materials often use stopping, ending, or cessation in practical explanation. | TOLERATE ALTERNATE | Keep quenching as the default, tolerate ending or cessation in controlled OSF-facing frames, refuse loose generic cessation drift. |
| sati | remembering | Dhammarato and OSF path-language strongly support remembering, while some rough glossary or coaching prose uses awareness-language. | ALIGN | Keep remembering as the default, tolerate awareness only as a controlled explanatory alternate, refuse mindfulness and bare-attention drift. |
| vimutti | release | OSF-facing prose can use liberation, but the repository default is sharper as release. | TOLERATE ALTERNATE | Keep release as the default, tolerate liberation as a controlled OSF-facing alternate, refuse generic spiritual-freedom rhetoric. |
| ānāpānasati | ānāpānasati | OSF glossary explains it as remembering to look at in-and-out breathing. | TOLERATE ALTERNATE | Keep the untranslated default, tolerate the OSF explanatory gloss, refuse collapse into generic breath-meditation language. |
| samādhi | unification of mind | Dhammarato and OSF materials strongly reject concentration and reinforce unification of mind. | ALIGN | Keep the current default and continue refusing concentration drift. |
| citta | feeling mind | OSF explanatory material can use mind, heart-mind, or center of consciousness, especially in practice prose. | TOLERATE ALTERNATE | Keep feeling mind as the default, tolerate OSF-facing explanatory alternates, refuse collapse into cognition or consciousness. |
| passaddhi | relaxation | Rough peace- or calm-language could easily flatten passaddhi into santi or generic tranquility. | REFUSE DRIFT | Keep relaxation as the default and refuse drift into undifferentiated peace-language. |
